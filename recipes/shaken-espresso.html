Shaken Espresso

Description

An espresso, simple syrup, and ice all mixed and shaken together

Ingredients:

  • Espresso
  • Simple Syrup
  • Ice
  • 2% milk

Steps:

  1. Put syrup, espresso shots, and ice into a cocktail shaker
  2. Shake
  3. Pour drink into cup
  4. Add desired amount of milk
  5. Enjoy
