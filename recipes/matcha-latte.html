Shaken Espresso

Description

Tea latte made with matcha green tea powder and sweetener

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup milk
  • 1-2 tsp matcha
  • 1 tbsp vanilla syrup
  • 1 cup ice

Steps:

  1. Place milk, matcha, and vanilla syrup together in a cup
  2. Mix well with spoon or a milk frother
  3. Once combined, add ice
  4. Enjoy
